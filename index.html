About Haus of Ash

Haus of Ash was born from fire and ritual — a modern apothecary of smoke, memory, and transformation. Every blend is an act of alchemy, weaving botanicals, resins, and woods into fragrances that feel both ancient and alive. More than perfume, each scent is a ritual: a way to claim space, summon presence, and carry embers of the sacred into the everyday.
